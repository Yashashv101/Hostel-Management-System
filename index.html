Our Features

Discover how HostelPals enhances hostel management and improves the student experience

Room Allocation

Efficient room allocation system that matches students with compatible roommates and preferred room types, minimizing conflicts and maximizing satisfaction.

Fee Management

Streamlined fee collection with automated reminders, multiple payment options, and real-time tracking of dues and payments for better financial control.

Communication Hub

Centralized platform for announcements, complaints, and feedback, ensuring seamless communication between students, wardens, and hostel administrators.

Mess Management

Digital meal planning, menu customization, and feedback system for mess services, helping improve food quality and reducing waste.

Analytics Dashboard

Visual analytics for occupancy rates, fee collection status, and student feedback, enabling data-driven decisions for hostel management.

Mobile Accessible

Fully responsive platform accessible on any device, allowing students and administrators to stay connected and manage hostel affairs on the go.
